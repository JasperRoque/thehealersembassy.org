Unlike standard business coaching, my approach integrates cosmic wisdom with practical business strategies to provide a truly holistic path forward for your business in the U.S. market.

My Unique Approach to Business Direction

What sets my Directional Guidance apart is the integration of three key elements:

  1. Practical Business Expertise: Drawing on extensive experience in U.S. business operations, market analysis, and strategic planning to provide actionable advice
  2. Astrological Insights: Utilizing cosmic patterns and energies to identify favorable timing for business moves and potential challenges to navigate

  2. Astrological Analysis: I create a business astrology chart that identifies cosmic influences affecting your business journey and optimal timing for key decisions.
